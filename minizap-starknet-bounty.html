Judging criteria

  • ▹ Originality and technical depth of the Starknet integration
  • ▹ Quality of public build logs (#MiniZap, clear demos)
  • ▹ UX, documentation, and how useful it is for others
  • ▹ Consistency across the bounty period
  • ▹ Alignment with Starknet strengths (scale, proofs, accounts)
